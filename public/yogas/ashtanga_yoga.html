Ashtanga Yoga

A dynamic and structured yoga practice that follows a specific sequence of postures and breathing techniques.

Benefits of Ashtanga Yoga

  • Enhances flexibility and strength.
  • Improves focus and discipline.
  • Boosts cardiovascular health.
  • Detoxifies the body through breath and movement.
  • Promotes mental clarity and inner peace.

Sun Salutation (Surya Namaskar)

Warms up the body and increases flexibility.

How to Perform:

Flow through 12 poses with synchronized breathing.

Triangle Pose (Trikonasana)

Improves balance and strengthens legs.

How to Perform:

Extend one leg, reach towards the foot, and stretch the opposite arm up.

Warrior Pose (Virabhadrasana)

Builds endurance and strengthens lower body.

How to Perform:

Step forward into a lunge, raise arms, and hold.

Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana)

Stretches the spine and calms the mind.

How to Perform:

Sit with legs extended, reach forward, and hold the toes.

Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)

Opens the chest and strengthens the back.

How to Perform:

Lie on your stomach, lift your upper body, keeping elbows slightly bent.

Boat Pose (Navasana)

Strengthens core muscles and improves balance.

How to Perform:

Sit, lift legs and arms, forming a V-shape.

Headstand (Sirsasana)

Improves circulation and focus.

How to Perform:

Use forearms to balance and lift legs straight up.

Seated Twist (Ardha Matsyendrasana)

Enhances spinal flexibility and detoxifies organs.

How to Perform:

Sit, bend one knee, place foot over the other leg, and twist.
