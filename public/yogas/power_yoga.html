Power Yoga

A dynamic and intense yoga practice that builds strength, endurance, and flexibility.

Benefits of Power Yoga

  • Improves cardiovascular health.
  • Builds strength and muscle tone.
  • Enhances flexibility and balance.
  • Boosts metabolism and aids weight loss.
  • Increases mental focus and reduces stress.

Downward-Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

Strengthens the arms, shoulders, and legs while improving flexibility.

How to Perform:

Start in a plank position, lift your hips up, and press your heels towards the floor.

Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II)

Develops leg strength, endurance, and stability.

How to Perform:

Stand with feet apart, bend the front knee, and extend arms parallel to the floor.

Chaturanga Dandasana (Four-Limbed Staff Pose)

Builds upper body strength, especially in the arms and core.

How to Perform:

Lower your body from a plank position, keeping elbows close to your ribs.

Crow Pose (Bakasana)

Improves arm strength and balance.

How to Perform:

Place your hands on the ground, lift your feet, and balance on your arms.

Boat Pose (Navasana)

Strengthens the core and improves balance.

How to Perform:

Sit, lift your legs, and balance on your sit bones while extending your arms forward.

Triangle Pose (Trikonasana)

Stretches the legs and torso while improving flexibility.

How to Perform:

Stand with legs wide, extend one arm down to the ankle and the other up.

Wheel Pose (Urdhva Dhanurasana)

Strengthens the spine and improves flexibility.

How to Perform:

Lie on your back, place hands near ears, and push up into a backbend.
