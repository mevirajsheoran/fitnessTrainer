Kundalini Yoga

A powerful yoga practice focused on awakening inner energy through breath, movement, and meditation.

Benefits of Kundalini Yoga

  • Enhances spiritual awareness and inner peace.
  • Increases energy and mental clarity.
  • Strengthens the nervous system.
  • Reduces stress and emotional blockages.
  • Balances chakras and promotes self-healing.

Spinal Flex

Improves spinal flexibility and energizes the body.

How to Perform:

Sit cross-legged, hold ankles, inhale arching spine forward, exhale rounding back.

Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)

Opens the heart and activates energy flow.

How to Perform:

Lie on your stomach, hands under shoulders, lift chest while keeping elbows slightly bent.

Frog Pose

Strengthens legs and activates lower chakras.

How to Perform:

Squat with heels up, fingertips on the floor, inhale lifting hips, exhale squatting down.

Sat Kriya

Balances energy and strengthens core muscles.

How to Perform:

Kneel, interlace fingers, raise arms overhead, chant "Sat" pulling navel in, "Nam" releasing.

Breath of Fire (Kapalabhati)

Detoxifies the body and increases lung capacity.

How to Perform:

Sit tall, inhale deeply, then rapidly exhale through the nose while pulling the navel in.

Eagle Pose (Garudasana)

Improves balance and mental focus.

How to Perform:

Wrap one leg around the other, cross arms in front, and hold the pose.

Shoulder Stand (Sarvangasana)

Stimulates the thyroid and increases blood circulation.

How to Perform:

Lie on your back, lift legs over head, support back with hands.

Root Lock (Mula Bandha)

Activates lower chakras and strengthens pelvic muscles.

How to Perform:

Sit cross-legged, inhale deeply, contract pelvic muscles, hold, then release.
