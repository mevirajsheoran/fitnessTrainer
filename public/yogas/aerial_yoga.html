Aerial Yoga

A fusion of traditional yoga and aerial arts that enhances flexibility, strength, and balance.

Benefits of Aerial Yoga

  • Improves flexibility and spinal decompression.
  • Enhances core strength and stability.
  • Reduces joint and spine pressure.
  • Boosts circulation and relieves stress.
  • Adds a fun and unique approach to fitness.

Inverted Butterfly

Relieves spinal tension and improves flexibility.

How to Perform:

Hang upside down in the hammock, keeping legs in a butterfly position.

Aerial Warrior Pose

Strengthens the legs and improves balance.

How to Perform:

Hold the hammock for support, extend one leg forward, and balance.

Flying Pigeon

Builds core strength and enhances flexibility.

How to Perform:

Rest one foot on the hammock, bend forward, and extend the back leg.

Aerial Plank

Activates the core and stabilizes muscles.

How to Perform:

Balance your feet on the hammock while holding a plank position.

Aerial Savasana

Promotes deep relaxation and relieves stress.

How to Perform:

Lie in the hammock in a cocoon-like position and relax.

Aerial Handstand

Improves upper body strength and balance.

How to Perform:

Use the hammock to support your hips while extending into a handstand.

Aerial Bow Pose

Enhances spinal flexibility and strength.

How to Perform:

Grip the hammock behind you, bend knees, and arch your back.
