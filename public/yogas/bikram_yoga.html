Bikram Yoga

A sequence of 26 postures practiced in a heated room, focusing on detoxification and flexibility.

Benefits of Bikram Yoga

  • Enhances flexibility and strength.
  • Detoxifies the body through sweating.
  • Improves circulation and lung capacity.
  • Helps in weight loss and stress relief.
  • Boosts focus and mental endurance.

Half Moon Pose (Ardha Chandrasana)

Improves balance and flexibility.

How to Perform:

Stand straight, raise arms, and bend sideways while keeping the core engaged.

Eagle Pose (Garudasana)

Strengthens the legs and improves concentration.

How to Perform:

Wrap one leg around the other and bring hands together in front of the chest.

Bow Pose (Dhanurasana)

Opens the chest and strengthens the back.

How to Perform:

Lie on your stomach, grab your ankles, and lift your chest while pulling your legs up.

Triangle Pose (Trikonasana)

Improves flexibility and stretches the spine.

How to Perform:

Stand with legs wide apart, extend one arm down to the ankle, and stretch the other upward.

Camel Pose (Ustrasana)

Enhances spinal flexibility and opens the chest.

How to Perform:

Kneel, arch your back, and reach for your heels with both hands.

Tree Pose (Vrikshasana)

Enhances balance and mental focus.

How to Perform:

Place one foot on the inner thigh of the opposite leg and balance with hands in prayer position.

Corpse Pose (Savasana)

Promotes deep relaxation and recovery.

How to Perform:

Lie on your back, relax your body, and focus on breathing.
