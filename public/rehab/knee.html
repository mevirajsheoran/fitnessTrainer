Knee Rehab Guide

Rehab Tips

# Tip Description
1 Use R.I.C.E Method Rest, Ice, Compression, and Elevation for knee recovery.
2 Strengthen Quadriceps Strong quads support the knee joint.
3 Improve Flexibility Gentle stretching prevents stiffness and improves mobility.
4 Wear Proper Footwear Good shoes provide support and reduce knee stress.

Rehab Exercises

# Exercise Description Image
1 Quad Sets Sit with legs straight, tighten thigh muscles.
2 Straight Leg Raise Lift one leg while keeping it straight.
3 Hamstring Curl Stand and bring your heel towards your buttocks.
4 Wall Sit Lean against a wall and hold a seated position.
5 Step-Ups Step up onto a low platform and back down.
6 Calf Raises Rise onto your toes and lower back down.
