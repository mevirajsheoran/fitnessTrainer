Shoulder Rehab Guide

Rehab Tips

# Tip Description
1 Maintain Proper Posture Keep shoulders back and avoid slouching.
2 Apply Ice/Heat Therapy Ice for inflammation, heat for stiffness.
3 Strengthen Rotator Cuff Stronger rotator cuff muscles reduce injury risk.
4 Stretch Regularly Improves flexibility and reduces stiffness.

Rehab Exercises

# Exercise Description Image
1 Pendulum Stretch Bend forward and let the injured arm swing gently in circles.
2 Wall Walk Stand close to a wall and walk your fingers upward.
3 External Rotation Use a resistance band and rotate the arm outward.
4 Internal Rotation Use a resistance band and rotate the arm inward.
5 Shoulder Blade Squeeze Pull shoulder blades together and hold.
6 Cross-Body Stretch Pull one arm across the chest to stretch the shoulder.
