Neck Rehab Guide

Rehab Tips

# Tip Description
1 Maintain Proper Posture Keep your head aligned with your spine; avoid hunching over.
2 Use Ice/Heat Therapy Ice for pain and swelling, heat for stiffness.
3 Take Regular Breaks Avoid prolonged sitting; move your neck frequently.
4 Sleep with Proper Support Use a firm pillow that supports the natural curve of your neck.

Rehab Exercises

# Exercise Description Image
1 Chin Tucks Sit upright and gently tuck your chin in towards your chest.
2 Neck Side Stretch Slowly tilt your head towards one shoulder until you feel a stretch.
3 Neck Rotations Gently turn your head left and right, holding for a few seconds.
4 Upper Trapezius Stretch Sit upright, gently pull your head sideways for a deep stretch.
5 Isometric Neck Resistance Press your palm against your forehead and resist movement.
6 Shoulder Shrugs Raise your shoulders up towards your ears and relax.
