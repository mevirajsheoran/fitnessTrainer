Running Training Guide

Running Tips

# Tip Description
1 Warm Up & Cool Down Always start with dynamic stretches and end with static stretches.
2 Maintain Good Running Form Keep your back straight, arms relaxed, and stride natural.
3 Stay Hydrated Drink water before and after your run to prevent dehydration.
4 Increase Distance Gradually Don't increase your weekly mileage by more than 10%.

Running Exercises

# Exercise Description Image
1 High Knees Run in place, bringing your knees to waist level.
2 Butt Kicks Jog in place while kicking your heels towards your glutes.
3 Leg Swings Hold onto a wall and swing your leg forward and backward.
4 Calf Raises Stand on your toes, hold, then lower back down.
