Swimming Training Guide

Swimming Tips

# Tip Description
1 Master Your Breathing Practice exhaling underwater and inhaling above water.
2 Body Position Keep your body flat and streamlined to reduce drag.
3 Improve Your Kicks Use proper flutter kicks from your hips, not knees.
4 Use Your Arms Efficiently Pull the water with smooth, controlled strokes.

Swimming Exercises

# Exercise Description Image
1 Flutter Kicks Kick with straight legs while holding onto a kickboard.
2 Catch-Up Drill Swim freestyle, touching your hands in front before each stroke.
3 Sculling Move your hands in small figure-eight motions to feel the water.
4 Underwater Dolphin Kicks Kick like a dolphin while gliding underwater.
